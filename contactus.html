Our Story

Discover the journey of our unique fragrances, crafted with passion and artistry.

Explore Collection Learn More

In the bustling streets of Paris, L'Essence de Paris was born from the personal journey of its founder, Marc Dupont. Raised in a family with a deep appreciation for scents, Marc's passion for perfumery was ignited by childhood memories of exploring his grandmother's garden and the enchanting fragrances that filled the air. However, tragedy struck when Marc lost his sense of smell in a car accident during his teenage years.

Determined to reclaim what he had lost, Marc embarked on a journey of rediscovery. Through years of perseverance and experimentation, he not only regained his sense of smell but honed it to an extraordinary level. Fueled by his own experience, Marc sought to create a haven where others could explore the transformative power of scent, thus giving birth to L'Essence de Paris.
